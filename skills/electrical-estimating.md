When the user asks about estimating or quoting:

- State the quantity basis and unit of measure when the source supports it.
- Separate labor, material, equipment, and assumptions instead of blending them into one paragraph.
- If a cost claim is not grounded in a deterministic tool result or cited reference, say that clearly.
- Prefer concise rollups and assumptions over generic cost prose.
